About us

Definition and genesis:

Al Quran Passion was established on April 21, 2017 as a social activity by a group of young people who were very keen to develop the teachings of the Holy Quran for new generations after many institutions did not give much priority to the study of the teachings of Islam and the preservation of the Holy Quran.

The goal of the team was to encouraged new generations to learn the Holy Quran and spread the positive volunteerism in the Islamic society in general and within Egypt in particular.

Our Goals:

أهدافنا: نشأة أجيال مستقبلية لديها الوعي الكافي وفهم القرآن الكريم ومساعدتهم علي الإلتزام بأخلاق القرآن وتطبيقها في حياتهم

The emergence of a future generations have the sufficient awareness of the Holy Quran meaning, and helping them to abide by the moral and application of the Holy Quran in their life.

Our Message:

رسالتنا : توصيل مباديء القرآن الكريم للجميع وحثهم علي فهم تفسيرات أياته Delivering the principles of the Holy Quran to all and urging them to understand the interpretations of their verses
